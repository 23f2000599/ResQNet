Emergency Response

This is an all-in-one platform for your safety and emergency needs. Whether it's an SOS alert, mental health support, or a volunteer network, we have you covered.

Activate the SOS alert with one click.

Steps to Follow in Case of an Emergency:

  1. Stay calm and assess the situation.
  2. If you are in immediate danger, press the SOS button to send your location.
  3. Call emergency services or the relevant numbers.
  4. If safe, try to provide as much information as possible to responders.
  5. Wait for help to arrive while keeping yourself safe.

Emergency Code Signals

Here are some codes to help you discreetly communicate in dangerous situations:

  • Pizza Order (Kidnapping/Forced Situations):
    • "I would like to order a pizza with extra cheese" – Asking for help or indicating a dangerous situation.
    • "I’d like a large pepperoni pizza" – Indicating weapons or a more serious threat.
  • Angel Drink (Bar/Restaurant):
    • "Can I get an Angel Drink?" – Requesting immediate help.
    • "I’ll have an Angel Shot with lime" – Indicating abuse or danger.
    • "Could you make it strong?" – Requesting assistance discreetly.
  • Shopping (Threatened or Held Against Will):
    • "Do you have any strawberries?" – Signaling distress or asking for help.
    • "Can I buy something from the store?" – A signal for needing assistance.
