About Us

At ResQNet, we are committed to building a safer and more connected world through innovative technology. Our platform offers an Emergency SOS System, where one-tap distress signals alert nearby responders, family, and authorities for swift assistance. The Crisis Marketplace provides quick access to essential supplies like food, medical kits, and emergency tools with proximity-based matching. SafeZone enhances security with AI-driven risk alerts, real-time location sharing, and emergency support, including a "Fake Call" feature for added safety. Prioritizing Women’s Safety, ResQNet enables instant crowd alerts, verified safe spaces, and a strong community-driven security system. With ResQNet, safety is just a tap away.
